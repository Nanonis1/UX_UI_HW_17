About Me

I became a designer at the age of 18, I liked to paint and draw most of the time. I started my studies designer in 2012, focusing more on the digital part of my career for about 4 years. Simultaneously, I worked as a social communicator, which developed new skills such as public speaking, writing, and teamwork. Speaking a second language has been an important part of my career to expand my design knowledge and have more opportunities in the field. I get energy from exploring and creating, always hungry for the unknown. The design always makes me feel excited about not knowing what the results will be, but constantly discovering and adjusting for the better.
